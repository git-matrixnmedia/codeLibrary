How it all started

The idea for Image App came about when Jalke Bugg was teaching graphic design programs at university and found students struggled to learn the basics. Partnering with Director Diana, the pair launched Fusion Books, an online design tool that made it easy for students and teachers to create their own yearbooks. They soon realised that the technology they'd developed could be used much more broadly. After searching far and wide, they launched Image app with help of Philippe and Gretchen.

We're a fully distributed team, meaning that we'll be fixing bugs and replying to your questions from around the world!

The Image App team works from multiple countries and continents. Living in a place that makes you happy is one of our core beliefs, and has stayed with the company since the beginning.
